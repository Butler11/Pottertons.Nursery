Welcome to pottertons Nursey

GREETINGS. We welcome visitors to the nursery TUESDAY to SATURDAY 10am to 3pm, 13 April to the end of September, at other times of the year by prior appointment. Social distancing rules to be firmly applied, limited to four people in the sales area. Our extensive sales area is restocked weekly, offering a huge range of Alpines, Dwarf Bulbs and Woodland plants. We have a beautiful 5 acre garden including a large rockery & pool, raised beds, crevice garden, troughs & woodland garden. Expert advice is freely available. Why not plan a visit soon.


We are a traditional family run specialist nursery which grows and supplies an extensive selection by MAIL ORDER and at the many shows or lectures we attend, throughout UK and Europe or directly from our nursery.


Breif History

The nursery was opened in 1971 and since then we have conducted our mail order business through our printed, fully descriptive catalogue in paper format, although the relatively recent boom in internet use has resulted in an increasing proportion of orders been taken through this web site. If you live in the UK and require a copy of our catalogue please send 6 x first class stamps with your address to the nursery.
